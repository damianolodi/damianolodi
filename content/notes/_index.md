This section is my way to collect all the information that I acquire from courses and readings in general, and that I would like to remember in time. For me, the process of writing things (at first by hand, and then sorting everything for a more structured text) is fundamental to learn new things.

_I hope that someone else will find those notes useful, at least as a first approach with a certain topic._ In each page, **I also list the most useful resources I used to learn those arguments,** so they can be referred from others to explore the topic more in-depth.

Watch out for errors, since I may miswrite something or even not understanding correctly. If you spot something, feel free to contact me so that I can correct the error! You can also add comments on each note page if you want to discuss the topic or add something useful.
