## Most Common Hotkeys
- A &rarr; add symbol.
- C &rarr; copy.
- R &rarr; rotate.
- G &rarr; drag component.
- M &rarr; move compinent or wire.
- W &rarr; start wiring.
- Space bar &rarr; place to 0 the cursor placement in the bottom-right part of the screen. Useful to measure distances.
