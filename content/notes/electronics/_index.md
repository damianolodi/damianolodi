## KiCad

KiCad is my go-to choice when it comes to EDA software. It is open-source, well supported, multi-platform and fairly powerful even for complicated PCB design.

In the following, I mainly report my notes from the [Udemy course](https://www.udemy.com/share/101XZU/) that I took to get started, with also my adjustment on the workflow and considerations about custom footprints design.

## PCB Design

PCB design is not as simple as it can seem, because lots of things should be considered, e.g. track width, thermal management, etc.

In the following, I report a collection of notes on the most diverse topics concerning PCB design. Each topic is mainly the summary of one or more application notes from some electronic manufacturers. Each application note will be properly referenced so that the original document can be reviewed in case more details are needed.
